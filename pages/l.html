About EduFlow

Revolutionizing education through innovative technology

At EduFlow, we believe that quality education should be accessible to everyone, everywhere. Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with pedagogical excellence to create an unparalleled learning experience.


Founded in 2020, we've grown from a small startup to a global platform serving millions of learners. Our AI-powered personalization engine ensures that every student receives a tailored learning journey that adapts to their unique needs and pace.


We're not just building software; we're shaping the future of education. Our team of educators, technologists, and designers work tirelessly to create tools that make learning engaging, effective, and enjoyable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about EduFlow

What makes EduFlow different from other LMS platforms?

+

EduFlow combines AI-powered personalization with a modern, intuitive interface. Our platform adapts to each learner's needs, provides real-time feedback, and offers advanced analytics that help educators optimize their teaching methods. We also prioritize mobile accessibility and gamification to keep learners engaged.

How much does EduFlow cost?

+

We offer flexible pricing plans to suit different needs. Our basic plan starts at $9.99/month per user, with discounts for educational institutions and bulk licenses. We also provide a free 30-day trial with full access to all features, no credit card required.

Is EduFlow suitable for corporate training?

+

Absolutely! EduFlow is designed to serve both academic and corporate learning needs. We offer specialized features for corporate training including compliance tracking, skill assessments, certification management, and integration with popular HR systems.

What kind of support do you provide?

+

We provide 24/7 customer support through multiple channels including live chat, email, and phone. Premium plans include dedicated account managers and priority support. We also offer comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and regular webinars to help you get the most out of our platform.

Can I integrate EduFlow with other tools?

+

Yes! EduFlow offers robust API access and pre-built integrations with popular tools like Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Zoom, Slack, and many more. Our open API allows you to create custom integrations to fit your specific workflow needs.
